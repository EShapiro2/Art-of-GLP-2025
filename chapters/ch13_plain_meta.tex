\chapter{Metaprogramming}
\label{ch:plain-meta}

This chapter introduces metaprogramming---programs that manipulate programs.

\section{Programs as Data}

In logic programming, programs are data: clauses are terms, goals are terms. This enables powerful metaprogramming.

\section{The Vanilla Meta-Interpreter}

\begin{verbatim}
solve(true).
solve((A, B)) :- solve(A), solve(B).
solve(A) :- clause(A, B), solve(B).
\end{verbatim}
